On patience and compound things

Edited 2 days ago · 3 backlinks · 4 links out

Everything good is slow. The garden we planted in March looks nothing like the one in the photograph — not yet. But there's a moment, usually in late June, when it suddenly resembles what we imagined, and then improves on it.

Yesterday @Maya Greenberg asked about the retainer proposal. I linked her to [[Proposal — Q2 partnership]] and said we'd circle back Monday. See also the conversation with @David Liu from last Tuesday — same pattern, different shape.

What I keep forgetting

  • The work you do when nobody is watching is the work.
  • Most "urgent" emails can wait a day; most actually wait a week.
  • When in doubt, [[Default to writing it down]].
"You are not obliged to complete the work, but neither are you free to desist from it." — Pirkei Avot 2:16

Tomorrow: review [[Sprint plan — week 17]] and reach out to @Noa Ben-Ari about the integration spec.
